How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

I'm having issues with in-app purchases

Please ensure you have a stable internet connection and are signed into your Apple ID. If the problem persists, try restarting the app or your device. Contact us if the issue continues.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel your subscription.

The app is not working properly

Try closing and reopening the app, or restart your device. Make sure you have the latest version installed from the App Store.
