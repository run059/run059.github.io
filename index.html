Troubleshooting Guide

App crashes or freezes

  • • Update to the latest version of the app
  • • Restart your device
  • • Free up storage space
  • • Update iOS to the latest version

Questions not loading

  • • Check your internet connection
  • • Force close and reopen the app
  • • Clear app cache in Settings
  • • Reinstall the app if needed

Purchase not recognized

  • • Use "Restore Purchases" in Settings
  • • Sign out and back into App Store
  • • Wait a few minutes and try again
  • • Contact us if issue persists

Statistics not updating

  • • Complete tests fully before exiting
  • • Check you're using the same account
  • • Sync from Settings > Sync Data
